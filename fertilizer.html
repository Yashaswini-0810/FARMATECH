Organic Fertilizer

Best for improving soil fertility and crop yield.

Compost

Rich in nutrients, improves soil structure, and enhances plant growth.

Vermicompost

Produced using earthworms, increases microbial activity in the soil.

Biofertilizer

Contains living microorganisms that boost soil fertility naturally.

Manure

Decomposed organic matter that enriches soil with essential nutrients.

Green Manure

Grown and plowed into the soil to improve fertility and organic matter.

Fish Emulsion

A liquid fertilizer made from fish, rich in nitrogen and trace minerals.

Seaweed Fertilizer

Derived from seaweed, provides micronutrients and stimulates plant growth.

Rock Phosphate

A natural source of phosphorus that improves root development.
